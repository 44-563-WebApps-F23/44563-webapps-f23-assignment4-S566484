B SOWMYA REDDY acts in a play

Ghosts , Henrik Ibsen

REGINA. Should you like me to take down his hat to him? MRS. ALVING. Has he not even got his hat on? REGINA. [Pointing to the hall.] No; there it hangs. MRS. ALVING. Let it be. He must come up now. I shall go and look for him myself. [She goes out through the garden door.] MANDERS. [Comes in from the hall.] Is not Mrs. Alving here? REGINA. She has just gone down the garden. MANDERS. This is the most terrible night I ever went through.
